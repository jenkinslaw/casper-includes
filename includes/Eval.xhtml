Thrive: A New Lawyer's Guide to Law Firm Practice

This book is for lawyers who are just beginning their careers, for lawyers who want to know what their role is in a law firm from day one and take their careers head-on, with direction and purpose.
11/14 2012

Blogging in One Hour for Lawyers

An effective blog can help you promote your practice, become more "findable" online, and take charge of how you are perceived by clients, journalists and anyone who uses the Internet. Blogging in One Hour for Lawyers will show you how to create, maintain, and improve a legal blog--and gain new business opportunities along the way. You will learn to: set up a blog quickly and easily, write blog posts that will attract clients, avoid ethics problems that may result from having a legal blog, and much more.
11/14 2012

Civic Fusion: Mediating Polarized Public Disputes

The book examines what mediators aspire to do, what they actually do, and outlines what needs to be done to bring disparate groups of people together to reach agreements on complicated public policy questions. This guide: will help you construct the metaphor of civic fusion and describe how passion, power, and conflict provide the energy for it, describes what it takes to build a foundation for civic fusion, and discusses other aspects of civic fusion.
11/14 2012

Twitter in One Hour for Lawyers

In just one hour, you may learn to: create a Twitter account and set up your profile, read tweets and understand Twitter jargon, rrite tweets--and send them at the appropriate time, engage with other Twitters users, and more.
